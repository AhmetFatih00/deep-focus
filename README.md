# 🍅 Deep Focus

Deep Focus is a gamified, clean, and highly interactive Pomodoro and Task Management application built with Flutter. It goes beyond a simple timer by acting as a personal productivity coach through gamification, streaks, and comprehensive analytics.

## ✨ Key Features

* **Gamification (Ranks & Streaks):** Level up your rank (Novice to Grandmaster) based on total focus time. Maintain your daily streak 🔥 to build strong study habits.
* **Daily Goal Tracker:** Create custom daily tasks with sub-tags. Watch the dynamic progress bars fill up as you complete your focus sessions.
* **Interactive Analytics:** Visualize your productivity with interactive bar charts (`fl_chart`). Filter your data by Day, Week, Month, or Year.
* **Advanced Theme Engine:** Switch instantly between carefully crafted UI themes (Clean Light, Deep Ocean, Midnight, Forest).
* **Local Notifications & Vibration:** Get alerted exactly when your focus session or break ends, even if the app is in the background.
* **Clean Architecture:** The codebase is structured using separation of concerns (Models, Views, Services, Theme) for high maintainability and scalability.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Libraries

* **Storage:** `shared_preferences` (Local key-value storage)
* **Charts:** `fl_chart`
* **Notifications:** `flutter_local_notifications`
* **Hardware:** `vibration`, `permission_handler`
